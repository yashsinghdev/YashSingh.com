Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about YASHNET AI.

What makes YASHNET AI different from other AI assistants?
YASHNET AI combines state-of-the-art natural language processing with specialized knowledge across multiple domains. Our AI understands context better, remembers conversation history, and offers more accurate and creative responses than standard AI assistants.
Is my data secure with YASHNET AI?
Absolutely. We employ enterprise-grade security measures including end-to-end encryption for all conversations. You own your data, and we never use your conversations to train our models without explicit permission.
Can I use YASHNET AI for commercial purposes?
Yes! Our Pro and Enterprise plans include commercial usage rights. You can use generated content commercially, though we recommend reviewing it for accuracy and originality as you would with any tool.
How does the free trial work?
Our free trial gives you access to all Pro features for 7 days with a limit of 50 requests per day. No credit card is required to start the trial, and you can upgrade at any time during or after the trial period.
What languages does YASHNET AI support?
YASHNET AI supports over 50 languages fluently, including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, and many more. The Starter plan includes 5 languages, while higher plans include all supported languages.

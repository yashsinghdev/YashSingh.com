Product Description

Our Premium Wireless Headphones deliver exceptional sound quality with active noise cancellation technology that blocks out external noise for an immersive listening experience. The over-ear design provides comfort for extended use, while the plush ear cushions create a perfect seal.

With Bluetooth 5.0 technology, enjoy seamless wireless connectivity up to 30 feet from your device. The built-in microphone allows for clear hands-free calls, and the intuitive touch controls let you manage music and calls with ease.

The 30-hour battery life ensures you can enjoy your music all day long, and the quick charge feature gives you 5 hours of playback with just a 10-minute charge. The foldable design makes these headphones perfect for travel.

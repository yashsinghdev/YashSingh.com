How to Play

Stone Paper Scissors is a classic hand game played between two players (in this case, you and the computer).

Rules:

  • Stone crushes scissors
  • Scissors cut paper
  • Paper covers stone

If both players choose the same option, it's a draw.

Click on one of the three options to make your move. The computer will randomly select its move, and the winner will be determined based on the rules above.
